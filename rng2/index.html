About Us

Idea that began as a thought between two friends, is in the process of transformation to be a nationwide recognized brand. R&G Fashions, not only is wonderfully stylish, luxurious and elegant, but also conveys an unique blend of sensuality, modernity and traditional Bengali style.

R&G Fashions offered top quality fabrics, sophisticated imaginative design and extremely comfortable wear; a coordinate system with individual components that were perfectly color-coordinated to allow mixing and matching several seasons of West Bengal
